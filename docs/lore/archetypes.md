# Archetypes

The live forge uses ten courier archetypes. In practice they function like schools, factions,
or professional lineages: each one carries a worldview, a stat identity, and a role in the City's conflict web.

---

## The Knights Technarchy
> *"The Dark Lights see everything. Serve or be disappeared."*

Cyber ninja zealots serving the secretive Dark Lights. They move sacred packages between hidden temples and are expected
to hunt Skids over the artifact run.

**Strengths:** Maximum Stealth, elite Speed. Excels in surveillance-heavy districts. Penalty: low Rep.

---

## Qu111s
> *"The truth is in the data. We will release it."*

A guerrilla journalist network protecting couriers like Skids while preparing a truth dump big enough to destabilize the UCA.

**Strengths:** Maximum Rep, high Grit. Thrives in Nightshade and open districts. Penalty: low Stealth.

---

## Iron Curtains
> *"Overthrow the oligarchy. By any means necessary."*

A public revolutionary front trafficking in weapons and leverage. Hidden beneath that myth is a UCA false-flag design.

**Strengths:** High Grit, balanced stats. Adaptable across all districts. Steady performers over specialists.

---

## Ne0n Legion
> *"Information is a commodity. We are the market."*

Thieves and mercenaries who treat every run as a profit opportunity. They move like couriers and think like opportunists.

**Strengths:** High Speed, strong Stealth, opportunistic Tech. Excels on smash-and-grab runs through neon districts.

---

## D4rk $pider
> *"Data for blackmail. Blackmail for survival."*

Dark-web operators using courier lanes for blackmail, scams, and survival. Their funding motives remain unclear.

**Strengths:** Maximum Tech, strong Stealth. The Grid is their natural habitat.

---

## The Asclepians
> *"Medicine moves. People live. No questions asked."*

Medical couriers and humanitarian smugglers moving medicine, organs, and restricted care through hostile territory.
They place Skids undercover after the Moonrisers notice him.

**Strengths:** Good Speed, high Grit, strong community access. Thrives in Batteryville and Nightshade.

---

## The Mesopotamian Society
> *"Indiana Jones on an electric mountain skateboard."*

Artifact couriers and academic treasure hunters moving relics for museums, collectors, and dangerous patrons.
Their work entangles Skids with the sacred object the Knights want.

**Strengths:** Maximum Rep, high Tech. Elite access to high-security archives. Penalty: limited Stealth.

---

## Hermes' Squirmies
> *"Any job. Any package. Any risk. Price adjusted accordingly."*

A neutral courier union whose entire value proposition is secrecy without ideology. If the job is real, they'll price it.

**Strengths:** Balanced stats. Neutral reputation. Works across all districts without faction penalties.

---

## UCPS
> *"Sanctioned delivery. Corporate rates. No questions."*

The official board-riding postal workforce of Airaway. UCPS roles double as some of the best cover identities in the setting.

**Strengths:** Good Speed, solid Rep. Moves through corp-controlled districts with reduced scrutiny.

---

## The Team
> *"Coordination wins races. Coordination wins everything."*

A neutral collective of ex-athletes whose synchronized operations make them competitive with the City's biggest powers.

**Strengths:** High Speed, high Grit. Exceptional in team-based operations. Strong Rep through visible performance.
